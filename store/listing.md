# TabWipe - Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name
TabWipe - Auto Cookie Cleaner

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Automatically delete cookies when you close tabs. Keep your browsing private with smart whitelist and one-click control.

## Description
🧹 TabWipe automatically cleans up cookies when you close browser tabs — the feature Chrome should have built in.

**The Problem:**
Every website drops cookies that track you across the web. Chrome's built-in "Clear on exit" is all-or-nothing — it nukes everything, including sites you want to stay logged into.

Cookie AutoDelete used to solve this, but Chrome's MV3 migration killed it. TabWipe fills that gap with a ground-up MV3 design.

**How it works:**
✅ Close a tab → TabWipe checks if you have other tabs open for that site
✅ No other tabs? → Cookies are automatically deleted after a short delay
✅ Whitelisted site? → Cookies are kept safe

**Free Features:**
• Automatic cookie cleanup on tab close
• Per-domain whitelist (synced across devices)
• Live cookie count badge on toolbar
• Quick stats: cookies deleted today & total
• One-click whitelist toggle in popup
• Dark/light mode support

**Pro Features:**
• Wildcard & regex pattern rules (*.google.com)
• Smart tracking cookie detection
• Keep session cookies, delete tracking cookies
• Scheduled cleanup (delete cookies older than X days)
• Per-cookie granularity (keep specific cookies from a domain)
• Import/export configuration
• Detailed statistics dashboard

**Privacy First:**
• No data collection. Zero analytics. No phone-home.
• All data stays in your browser (chrome.storage)
• Open source: https://github.com/4ier/tabwipe

**Permissions Explained:**
• cookies: Read and delete cookies (core functionality)
• tabs: Know when tabs open/close (trigger cleanup)
• storage: Save your whitelist and settings
• alarms: Reliable background scheduling
• host_permissions: Access cookies for all sites

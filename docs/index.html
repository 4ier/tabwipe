FAQ

Is this the replacement for Cookie AutoDelete?

Yes. CAD stopped working when Chrome forced the MV3 migration. TabWipe is built from scratch for MV3 — no legacy code, no workarounds.

Will I get logged out of everything?

Only sites you don't whitelist. Add your email, bank, and frequently used sites to the whitelist in one click — those cookies are never touched.

What about service worker limitations?

TabWipe never relies on in-memory state. Every time the service worker wakes up, it rebuilds its tab tracking from chrome.tabs.query(). A fallback alarm sweep runs every 5 minutes to catch anything missed.

Do you collect any data?

Zero. No analytics, no telemetry, no phone-home. All data stays in your browser. The code is open source — verify it yourself.
